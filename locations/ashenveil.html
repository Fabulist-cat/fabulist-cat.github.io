Ashenveil

Capital of the Northern Reaches

Type City-state
Population ~40,000
Ruler The Mist Council
Founded Third Age, year 212

Ashenveil is built on a plateau where three rivers converge, perpetually wrapped in low cloud. The city's famous white-stone spires emerge from the mist at dawn, giving rise to the traveller's saying: Ashenveil shows you its crown before its face.

"We arrived at dusk. The mist was so thick we heard the bells of the Gate District before we saw the walls. My horse refused to proceed until I lit a lantern."

— Sera Vondrath, Journals of the Northern Road
